A Smile To Their Faces

Street children in Bangladesh live in extreme poverty, and do not have sufficient access to human rights such as education, housing, healthcare, and protection. So this project works with street children, civil society and the government to create change and improve the quality of street children’s lives and their access to education, healthcare, shelter and safe employment opportunities.
